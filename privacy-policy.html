Privacy Policy

For the Third Eye Android mobile application providing attendance, payroll, and location tracking services.

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Welcome to Third Eye, developed by Third Eye Assurance Private Limited. The company is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android mobile app for attendance and payroll services. By using our mobile app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the app.

01 Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following personal information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, residential address, phone number, PAN, Aadhar number, family and nominee details, and employee identification number.
  • Attendance Data: Check-in and check-out times with selfie, location information, and other relevant attendance-related information.
  • Location Data: Our app includes a location tracing feature that collects and stores your device's geographical location to enhance your experience and provide location-based services.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and network information.
  • Payroll Information: Salary details, bank account information for direct deposit, tax information, and other relevant payroll-related data.
  • Usage Information: Non-personal information about how you use our app, such as the features you interact with, duration of usage, and any errors encountered.

02 How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: Tracking attendance, processing payroll, and generating relevant reports.
  • Location Data: Providing location-based services, marking attendance, and analyzing and improving app performance.
  • To Communicate: Sending you service updates and important notices.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: Using and disclosing information as required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Device Information: Ensuring compatibility, optimizing performance, identifying technical issues, and enhancing security.
  • To Improve Our Services: Analyzing usage patterns and enhancing app functionality.

03 Data Security
