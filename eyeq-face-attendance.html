Privacy Policy

For EyeQ Face Attendance — a face-recognition attendance application in which registered faces are matched on the device and attendance is controlled by your organisation's administrator.

Last updated: 04 July 2026

Welcome to EyeQ Face Attendance ("the App"), developed by Third Eye Assurance Private Limited ("we", "us", "our", or "the Company"). The App lets organisations record employee attendance using on-device face recognition. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles, how it is used, and the controls available to you. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here. Please read it carefully.

Privacy at a glance

  • Faces are registered and matched on the device — not on our servers.
  • Employee records come from your organisation's web portal, not from us.
  • Attendance is synced only to your organisation's own web portal.
  • The administrator can clear all data from the device at any time.
  • We never sell, rent, or share your data with any third party.

01 How the App Works

The App is designed around an administrator-controlled workflow. Understanding this flow makes the rest of this policy clear:

  1. Your organisation registers its employees on its own web portal.
  2. An authorised administrator signs in to the App.
  3. The administrator restores (downloads) the employee list from the web portal to the device.
  4. The administrator registers each employee's face — or removes a previously registered face — on the device.
  5. The administrator signs out and returns to the Home screen.
  6. On the Home screen, employees mark attendance (punch) using face recognition.
  7. To upload attendance, the administrator signs in again and syncs the punch data back to the web portal.
  8. The administrator can clear all data from the device at any time.

Beyond the steps above, the App does not collect any personal information.

02 Information the App Handles

The App handles only the information required for the workflow above:

  • Employee information: Details supplied by your organisation's web portal — such as employee name and identification number, as configured by your organisation — restored to the device by the administrator.
  • Facial data (biometric): When the administrator registers an employee, the App captures the employee's facial image and/or a mathematical representation (face template) of it, used solely to recognise that employee during attendance. This is stored on the device.
  • Attendance records: The date and time of each punch and the matched employee, kept on the device until synced to the web portal.
  • Administrator credentials: Used to authenticate the administrator's access to the App and to the web portal.
  • Basic device information: The App uses standard device and camera functions to operate. Any such information is used only to run the App on your device.

03 Facial & Biometric Data

Because face recognition involves biometric information, we treat it with particular care:

  • Purpose only: Facial data is used only to identify enrolled employees when they punch attendance. It is not used for any other purpose.
  • Stored on the device: Face registration and matching are performed on the device. Attendance synced to the web portal contains the attendance record (such as employee and time); the facial image or template itself is not required to be transmitted for this to work.
  • Consent: Employees should be informed before their face is registered. Your organisation is responsible for obtaining any consent required for face registration under applicable law.
  • Removal: The administrator can remove any registered face at any time, and can clear all facial data from the device.

04 How the Information Is Used

  • To register employees and recognise them for attendance.
  • To record and display attendance punches.
  • To sync attendance data to your organisation's web portal at the administrator's request.
  • To authenticate the administrator.
  • To operate, maintain, and troubleshoot the App.

We do not use your information for advertising, tracking, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to attendance.

05 Where Data Is Stored

  • Employee records and facial data are stored locally on the device.
  • Attendance data stays on the device until the administrator syncs it to the web portal.
  • Data synced to the web portal is stored and controlled by your organisation.
  • We do not maintain a separate copy of your organisation's employee or attendance data outside the App and your organisation's own systems.

06 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party. Information flows only between the device and your organisation's own web portal.

We may disclose information only where required to do so by law, or to protect our legal rights, safety, or property.

07 Data Retention & Deletion

  • The administrator can clear all data — employee records, facial data, and attendance punches — from the device at any time using the in-app option.
  • Uninstalling the App removes the App's data from the device.
  • Data held on your organisation's web portal is governed by your organisation's own retention practices.

08 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information handled by the App, including on-device storage protections and secured communication with the web portal. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

09 Permissions the App Uses

  • Camera: to capture faces for registration and to recognise employees during attendance.
  • Storage: to keep employee records, facial data, and attendance data on the device.
  • Internet / Network: to restore employee data from, and sync attendance data to, your organisation's web portal.

10 Administrator Responsibilities

Administrative access is restricted to authorised personnel of the organisation. Administrators are responsible for obtaining employee consent for face registration where required, keeping login credentials confidential, and clearing data from any device that is lost, reassigned, or no longer in use.

11 Children's Privacy
